Correct Computer Assembly Steps

1 April 2023.

If you want to assemble your own computer, we need to know the steps. Oh yes, before that we have to understand the preparations before assembling a computer that has been described in the previous article.

Because the preparation of each component is done by themselves, it is important for anyone to know the tools and materials as well as the various steps that must be carried out. It is intended that the computer assembly is successful and can be operated optimally

To find out how to assemble a computer and its tools and materials, see the following explanation:

1. Open the case on the CPU

Unscrew the captives that are on the back of the CPU case. Then, remove the back cover of the casing.

2. Attach the processor to the motherboard

At this stage, you need to make sure the type of processor used. Because, this relates to the type of socket that will be used. Start by opening the processor socket. Carefully pull the hook to the left. Then, pull up.

After that, open the iron cover on the processor Then, insert the processor by holding the edges with three fingers. At this stage, you have to be careful and don't touch the golden circle (pin) so that no dirt sticks to it. If it feels right, you can enter the processor. After that, attach the safety by pressing the iron hook or lock carefully.
